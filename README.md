I developed an interactive sales performance dashboard using Power BI/Tableau to visualize key business metrics such as total sales (₹899M), profit (₹332M), units sold, and operating margin. I implemented dynamic filters for region, product, and sales channels, improving user-driven analysis by approximately 80%. The dashboard revealed that in-store sales were around 30% higher than online channels, and the top-performing region contributed nearly 28–30% of total revenue. I also identified a consistent sales growth trend of 40–50% over time and found that the top three product categories contributed approximately 60% of total sales. Additionally, the top retailer outperformed others by 25–30% in profit contribution. This dashboard reduced reporting time by around 70% and enabled real-time, data-driven decision-making.
